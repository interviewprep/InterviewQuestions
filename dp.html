A robber cannot steal money from adjacent houses because they will wake up to the noise. If he steals from house i, he cannot steal from i-1 or i+1. For example, if there are 3 houses (0, 1, 2), he can steal in house 0 and house 2, or just house 1. 
 
You have an array where array[i] is the money in house i. Calculate the maximum amount money he can earn if he wants to steal from these houses as per the above mentioned condition.
